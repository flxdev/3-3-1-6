About Bureau

Architectural bureau 33|16 was founded in 2009 by architects Oleg Kapustin and Evgenii Daineko. The Bureau operates on the international market in the field of architecture, interior design, landscape design and project management of projects. To date, the company employs 25 staff, geography projects involves Belarus, Russia and Europe.
